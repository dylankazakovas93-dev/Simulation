σ is ONE thing: how far trades are displaced from their historical order (Gaussian reorder of the sequence). It does not touch win rate, sizes, or frequency. σ=0 replays the exact historical order; σ≥2 ≈ fully shuffled. Same seed ⇒ identical results.
Cushion = TOTAL distance kept above the failure threshold after a payout. A firm's own required buffer (Apex safety-net, TPT buffer zone) counts toward it — never double-charged.
